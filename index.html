Our Story

Born in the
Township,
Built for the World

Rands Cape Town is more than a restaurant — it is a declaration. Situated in the heart of Khayelitsha, we are proof that world-class hospitality, premium food, and electric entertainment belong in every neighbourhood.

"We are challenging the notion of what comes out of the township. You don't have to go all the way to town to get the treatment you deserve."
— Mfundo, Co-Founder

From signature braai platters and chakalaka to headline DJs and celebrity appearances, every visit to Rands is a full sensory experience rooted in South African culture, pride, and excellence.

Our Heritage

From Khayelitsha
To The World

2015

The Beginning

Born from a vision to bring world-class hospitality to Khayelitsha, Rands started as a small local spot with big dreams.

2018

Community Roots

Became a local institution, known for authentic braai culture and supporting township artists and musicians.

2020

National Recognition

Featured in major publications and became a destination for food lovers seeking authentic South African experiences.

2023

Global Impact

International visitors and celebrities discovered Rands, putting Khayelitsha on the global culinary map.
